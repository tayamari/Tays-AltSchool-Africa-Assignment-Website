Biography

Hi! My name is Tay Little. I'm a student of frontend engineering and UX design. I love coding, learning about it is fascinating to me. I aspire to create amazing and useful designs that are accessible to people and improve the world.

Why did I join AltSchool?

I joined AltSchool Africa because I was drawn in by the prioritization of Africans joining tech. Because of disenfranchisement, career-wise, the tech world is overwhelmingly white, cis, and male-dominated. I believe it's important for me to follow my passions in this field, and AltSchool seemed like a possible way of helping to achieve that.

Goals

My goal in the School of Engineering program is to build confidence with my use of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and any other languages we get exposed to.
